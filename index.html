What we do One service, five phases
We build AI automation systems

Most AI projects fail because they start with the technology, not the problem. We do it the other way around — understand the workflow first, then design an AI system that fits it. The result is automation your team trusts and actually uses.

01 Business Process Modelling Map workflows, roles, systems, decisions and bottlenecks using BPMN 2.0 — in a format your whole team can read and validate. Phase 1
02 AI Automation Design Identify exactly where AI should assist, automate, validate, summarize or generate — and where humans must stay in control. Phase 2
03 Custom AI Agents & Human-in-the-Loop UI Develop controlled agents alongside the review and approval interface — prompts, validations and fallbacks are built together with the dashboards that keep people in control of every output. Phase 3
04 Data Flow Integration Connect your systems via APIs, databases and files so that data moves reliably through every step without manual handling. Phase 4
05 Deployment & Optimization Pilot, test, monitor and continuously improve — from first prototype to a system running reliably in production. Phase 5
